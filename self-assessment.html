Self-Assessment

An honest checklist to see where you are today and find your smallest next step. There are no grades. Come back every few months — the shape changes.

Tick what’s true for you today — not what you intend to do, not what you did once last quarter. Your ticks are saved in your browser so you can come back over time. Nothing is sent anywhere.

When you’re done, the summary at the bottom will point you at the area most worth your next hour.

🏆

68–83: You’re operating well ahead of the stage. Look at the items you didn’t tick — they’re specific growth edges. Your next step is probably the Craft & Ownership guide — features end-to-end and raising the craft bar for the team.

📈

50–67: Solid. You’ve built the core habits. Pick the three unticked items that feel most real, and work on one each month. Usually Questioning, Speaking Up, Shipping Safely, or Working With Others is where the next unlock lives.

⚙️

28–49: You’re a capable coder, growing. The next step is less about writing more code and more about the habits around it — completion, questioning, speaking up, shipping safely. Start with Foundations this week; pick one page a week after.

💡

0–27: This guide is for you. Don’t try to change everything at once — that’s how people abandon growth plans. Read Foundations this week. Pick one habit. Come back in a month and re-take this.
